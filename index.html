What does Web mean? The Web is the common name for the World Wide Web, a subset of the Internet consisting of the pages that can be accessed by a Web browser. ... However, the term Internet actually refers to the global network of servers that makes the information sharing that happens over the Web possible.

HTML elements are the building blocks of HTML pages. With HTML constructs, images and other objects, such as interactive forms, may be embedded into the rendered page.
